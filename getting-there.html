--- title: Getting to IC2S2 ---
Arrive at the Conference

The central conference venue is the Dudley H. Davis Center (590 Main St, Burlington, VT 05401) on the University of Vermont campus in downtown Burlington. The venue is within walking distance of downtown hotels and amenities.

Access from Major US/European Cities

Burlington International Airport (BTV) is our hub for domestic and international travel and is a convenient 10 minutes from campus by car or shuttle. BTV can be reached with direct flights from NYC, Chicago, Philadelphia, Atlanta, Charlotte, Detroit, Newark, Orlando, Toronto City, and Washington DC. International participants can also opt to fly into Montreal Trudeau (YUL) Airport, a 1.5 hour drive from Burlington in the Quebec province of Canada (accessible by bus or car), or any major airport in the metropolitan New York area - Burlington is accessible from New York City by a scenic Amtrak train that provides wifi and arrives at the Burlington waterfront within walking distance of downtown hotels.

Arrive via Plane

From Burlington International Airport (BTV)

Burlington International Airport is just 10 minutes from the UVM campus. Direct flights are available from:

  • New York City (NYC/EWR)
  • Chicago (ORD)
  • Philadelphia (PHL)
  • Atlanta (ATL)
  • Charlotte (CLT)
  • Detroit (DTW)
  • Orlando (MCO)
  • Toronto City (YTZ)
  • Washington DC (IAD/DCA)

Ground transportation from BTV to campus includes taxis, rideshare services, and shuttle services.

From Montreal Trudeau Airport (YUL)

International participants may find convenient connections through Montreal Trudeau Airport (YUL), located approximately 1.5 hours from Burlington. Ground transportation options include rental cars and bus services between Montreal and Burlington.

From New York Area Airports

Participants can fly into any major New York metropolitan area airport (JFK, LaGuardia, Newark) and take the scenic Amtrak train to Burlington. The Amtrak Vermonter and Ethan Allen Express routes provide wifi service and arrive at the Burlington waterfront station, within walking distance of downtown hotels and a short trip to campus.

Parking

On-Campus Guests: Parking for guests staying on campus is currently $3.00 per day.

Commuting Guests: Parking for guests commuting to campus (whether local attendees, hotel guests, etc.) is $8.00 per day in the lots closest to meeting venues, or $4.00 per day in lots a short walk away.

Street Parking: Street parking is ample during the summer months and is available adjacent to the UVM campus. Both free options and city parking meters (available through the ParkMobile App) provide accessible parking within a ten-minute walk of campus locations.
