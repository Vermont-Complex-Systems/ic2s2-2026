On-Campus Housing

For highly discounted accommodations, campus housing is available. UVM offers several levels of overnight accommodations. Our 2025 rates are listed below; generally our rates increase about 3% annually, but we have confirmed if a contract is signed before May we can hold our 2025 housing prices. Rates do not include 12% housing tax. Linen package includes sheets, pillow and pillowcase, blanket, bath towels, hand towel, and washcloth.

University Heights Complexes

Air-conditioned single and double occupancy rooms with semi-private bathrooms (one bathroom shared between 2-4 people). Rates include linen package:

  • Single: $82.00 per person
  • Double: $59.00 per person

Central Campus Residence Hall

Air-conditioned single and double occupancy rooms with centrally located private bathrooms:

  • Single: $51.00 per person ($62.00 with linens)
  • Double: $45.00 per person ($56.00 with linens)

Standard Housing

Non-air-conditioned single and double occupancy rooms with centrally located bathrooms:

  • Single: $43.00 per person ($54.00 with linens)
  • Double: $37.00 per person ($48.00 with linens)

On campus housing registration can be incorporated into your conference registration for people who would like an affordable, convenient housing option.
